How to Use

Mat Mode (Default): Place phone flat on mat (screen up) where your head touches. Tap the screen with your nose/head at each Sajdah.

Pocket Mode: Keeps phone in pocket. Screen must stay on for sensors to work.

Logic: 2 Sajdahs = 1 Rakah. Works for standing and sitting prayers.
