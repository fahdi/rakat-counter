Place phone in pocket or on prayer mat. App will automatically count rakahs from your movements.

Easy Guide

1. Pick a Mode: Use Mat Mode if the phone is on the floor, or Pocket Mode if it's in your pocket.

2. How it Counts: The app counts 1 Rakah every time you finish two prostrations (putting your head down).

3. Well-Lit Room: For the mat mode to work automatically, please pray in a well-lit room. The app needs to see the light so it knows when your head covers the phone.

4. No Tapping: Once you start, you don't need to touch the phone. Just pray naturally!
