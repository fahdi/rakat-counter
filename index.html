Easy Guide

1. Pick a Mode: Use Mat Mode if the phone is on the floor, or Pocket Mode if it's in your pocket.

2. How it Counts: The app counts 1 Rakah every time you finish two prostrations (putting your head down).

3. Place Phone on Mat: Put your phone face-up on your prayer mat where your head will touch during Sajdah.

4. No Tapping: The app detects the vibration when your head touches the mat. Just pray naturally!
