How to Use

Mat Mode: Place phone flat on mat (screen up). Tap the screen with your head/nose at each Sajdah.

Pocket Mode: Keeps phone in pocket. Detects movements automatically.

Rule: 2 Sajdahs = 1 Rakah. This works for standing and sitting prayers.
